## Decision Rules

Record a decision here when:

- The operator approves an architectural direction.
- The operator approves a deviation from default framework behavior.
- The operator authorizes proceeding with blockers.
- The operator approves a partial build.
- The operator approves an override.
- A durable convention is introduced for future agents.

Do not record routine implementation details here.

Do not record secrets, credentials, tokens, or paid portal access details.
